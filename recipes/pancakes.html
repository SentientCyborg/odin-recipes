Pancakes

Pancakes have always been a breakfast favorite, but the poplularity of instant pancake batter has made homemade pancakes a dream of the past. Fortunately for us, making pancakes from scratch is a relatively easy process.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1/4 tsp of salt

Directions:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the wet together.
  3. Create a well in the bowl of dry ingredients and pour the wet ingredients in.
  4. Whisk until the batter is smooth.
  5. Using a griddle on medium heat, pour approximately 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the griddle and brown on both sides.
  6. Serve with toppings of choice.
